About Me

I am a Graphic designer, specializing in fresh takes on page layout and minimal design, with ten years of print industry experience, including 15 years of client management, generating new designs, re-thinking outdated designs, and prepress. I have a thorough knowledge of all Adobe design programs, and of all printing processes. Exceptional customer rapport, repeatedly a sought out point of contact for customers who know that they can trust to ensure their pieces turn out as well as imagined. Degree in graphic design and continuing education in digital and web design.

I am currently working at Arzberger Stationers. Arzberger is a nearly 100 year old engraving company, specializing in custom personal stationery and wedding invitations, with representatives nation wide. My customer service role involves pairing with representatives to bring their clients ideas to life. The needs vary from custom monogram creation, to thoughtful pairings of typography, to creative use of color. Orders are handled from start to finish, including typesetting, layout, prepress, and follow up throughout the production process.
